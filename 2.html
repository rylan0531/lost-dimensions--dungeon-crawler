To publish to the official Apple App Store, Apple requires a $99/year developer fee. There is unfortunately no way to bypass this if you want your game to appear in the "App Store" search results.

However, you can do it completely for free by turning your game into a PWA (Progressive Web App).

What is a PWA?

It allows your users to visit a website and click "Add to Home Screen." The game then appears on their phone with its own icon, hides the browser bar, and feels exactly like a real app from the store.

To make your game "Installable" for free, follow these steps:

1. Update the Code

We need to add a manifest.json file and update your index.html so phones recognize it as an app.

2. How to "Publish" for Free

  1. Host it for free: Use a service like Vercel, Netlify, or GitHub Pages. You just upload your code folder, and they give you a link (like lost-realms.vercel.app).

  2. Open on iPhone: Open that link in Safari.

  3. Install: Tap the Share button (the square with an arrow) and scroll down to tap "Add to Home Screen."

  4. Play: A "Lost Realms" icon will appear on your phone. When you tap it, it opens in full-screen without any browser bars, just like an app from the App Store.

Why this is better for now:

  • $0 cost: No Apple fees.

  • No Review: Apple takes 27 days to "review" apps. PWAs update instantly whenever you change your code.

  • Shareable: You can just text the link to your friends, and they can "install" it immediately.
